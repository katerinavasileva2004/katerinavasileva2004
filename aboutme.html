About Me

My name is Katerina Vasileva, I am a software engineering student from Skopje. Growing up, I was always curious and eager to learn new things. This curiosity led me to explore different fields, from technology and science to arts and humanities. As a result, I have cultivated a broad skill set that enables me to approach problems from multiple perspectives. In my free time, I enjoy pursuing my hobbies, which range from drawing and photography to coding and gaming. These activities not only bring me joy but also provide me with new insights and inspiration for my future profession.

Technology has always fascinated me. I enjoy staying updated with the latest advancements and tinkering with gadgets and software. This interest has naturally led me to develop skills in programming and web development, which I continuously strive to improve.

Below you'll find a list of infomation about me, such as education, hobbies and skills. Feel free to explore my webpage to learn more about me!
